Content :American Airlines Group ( AAL ) and JetBlue Airways ( JBLU ) announced plan to terminate their interline traffic agreement and reciprocal frequent flyer program accrual agreement, which includes select routes from Boston Logan International Airport and New York's John F. Kennedy International Airport. Beginning March 10, each airline will no longer accept new interline sales for travel on the other carrier. Effective April 1, customers will no longer earn miles or points when traveling on eligible routes operated by the other airline, the companies said.
