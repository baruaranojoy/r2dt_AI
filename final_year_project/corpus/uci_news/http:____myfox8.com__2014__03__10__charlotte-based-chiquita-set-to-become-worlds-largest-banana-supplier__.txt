Content :Chiquita center in uptown Charlotte (WSOC) Chiquita center in uptown Charlotte (WSOC) CHARLOTTE, N.C. — Charlotte-based Chiquita is planning to buy an Ireland company to become the world’s largest banana supplier. The all-stock deal between Chiquita and Fyffes is valued at $529 million, according to WSOC . The combined company will be called Chiquita-Fyffes. Chiquita’s CEO said they will offer their customers more diverse products, while maintaining their brands. The corporate office will remain in Charlotte, and there will be another one in Dublin.
