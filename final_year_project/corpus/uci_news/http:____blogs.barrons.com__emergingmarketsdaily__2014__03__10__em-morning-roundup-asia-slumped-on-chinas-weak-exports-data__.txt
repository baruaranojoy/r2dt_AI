Asian markets slumped today, after China reported over the weekend weaker-than-expected February exports data . February exports slumped 18% from a year ago, surprising the market which had expected a 5% increase. Asia equities slumped today on renewed concerns over a China slowdown. The Hong Kong Hang Seng Index was down 1.7%, the Shanghai Composite Stock Market Index slumped 2.9%. Taiwan and South Korea followed China lower as well. The Taiwan TAIEX Index lost 0.6%. The KOSPI Composite Index retreated 1%.
