Content :Pizza chain Sbarro has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection for the second time in three years. The tourist favorite has 1,000 to 5,000 creditors, $100 million to $500 million in assets and $100 million to $500 million in estimated liabilities, according to a document filed in Manhattan Federal Court. The move comes as the Melville, L.I., company seeks to reorganize due to flagging sales. Sbarro recently announced the closure of about 150 restaurants. The Melville, L.I., company recently announced it would close 150 restaurants and it seeks to get its finances in order. (Jay LaPrete/Bloomberg)
