Content :President Donald Trump resumed his Twitter onslaught against former senior White House advisor and "Apprentice" contestant Omarosa Manigault Newman, calling her a "dog" in his most vicious attack on his former protege yet. The president's tweet came the morning after Manigault-Newman told MSNBC's Chris Matthews that she would cooperate with Robert Mueller if she is contacted again.
